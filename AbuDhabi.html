Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi is the capital and second most populous city in the United Arab Emirates. Abu Dhabi lies on a T-shaped island jutting into the Persian Gulf from the Centeral western coast.

Crown Plaza Hotel

Situated in Abu Dhabi central business district, this 5-star hotel offers spacious rooms with panoramic city views, just a 12-minute walk from the Corniche. Facilities include an 18-metre rooftop pool. Including free Wi-Fi, the air-conditioned rooms of the Crowne Plaza Abu Dhabi have parquet floors and modern furnishings. All rooms are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The private bathrooms include luxury amenities. The Crowne Plaza offers 7 different dining options, including Cho Gao, Spaccanapoli and Heroes. The Level Lounge is the hotel’s trendy bar, which features international DJs and exotic cocktails. Guests can enjoy Swedish, Thai, and Shiatsu massages in one of the 3 massage rooms available. The Crowne Plaza also offers body treatments and has a sauna, a gym, and hot tub. The Crowne Plaza Abu Dhabi is located only 1.5 km from Marina Mall and is 4 km from the Abu Dhabi Mall. A public beach is situated one km away. The hotel offers 24-hour front desk service and free on-site private parking.

Al Jazeera Royal Hotel

Al Jazira Royal Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen TV, an on-site restaurant and guest laundry facilities. Located in Madinat Zayed, Abu Dhabi Mall is 3.5 km away. All soundproofed rooms feature a seating area and a minibar. The kitchen is equipped with fridge and a tea/coffee maker and joins up with a small dining area. Arabic and international delicacies are served in the hotel’s restaurant. Al Jazira Royal also has room service and a 24-hour front desk. Al Jazira Royal Hotel is 1.5 km away from the beachside attractions at Corniche Road.
